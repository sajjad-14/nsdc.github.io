We are NSDC-ASEB!

We are the National Student Data Corps (NSDC) at Amrita School of Engineering, dedicated to empowering students through hands-on experience in data science. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and professional growth while addressing real-world challenges.


Through workshops, hackathons, and networking events, we aim to enhance technical skills and connect students with industry experts. By promoting research and community outreach, we strive to create a vibrant ecosystem that prepares members for successful careers in the ever-evolving field of data science.

Events

Data Innovation Summit

This flagship event serves as a premier platform for students to showcase their innovative data science projects. It features keynote speeches from industry leaders, panel discussions, and workshops on emerging trends in data science.

Innovation Lab Nights

Regular ideation events held every few months where students pitch ideas addressing significant global challenges. Participants receive feedback from faculty and peers, forming teams to develop these ideas into viable projects.

InnoThon: The Data Innovation Hackathon

A competitive hackathon where students transform ideas generated during Innovation Lab Nights into functional prototypes. Teams collaborate under expert mentorship to tackle real-world challenges using data analytics and machine learning.

Machine Learning Workshops

The Machine Learning Workshop by the National Student Data Corps (NSDC) provides students with hands-on experience in essential machine learning concepts through interactive sessions on data preprocessing and model evaluation, guided by industry experts.
